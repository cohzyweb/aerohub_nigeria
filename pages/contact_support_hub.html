Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about AeroHub Nigeria

International visitors need a valid passport with at least 6 months validity and a Nigerian visa (unless from visa-exempt countries). Yellow fever vaccination certificate is mandatory. Business travelers may need additional documentation.

We recommend arriving 3 hours before international flights and 2 hours before domestic flights. During peak periods (holidays, major events), consider arriving 30 minutes earlier.

We offer premium lounges, VIP assistance, secure parking, luxury ground transportation, special assistance for disabled passengers, and business aviation services. All can be booked through our Services Marketplace.

Lounge access is available through airline status, credit card benefits, or direct purchase (₦15,000). Book online for guaranteed access and special rates. Our lounges feature Nigerian cuisine, business facilities, and cultural showcases.

Experience Nigerian art galleries, traditional music performances, local craft shops, authentic cuisine restaurants, and cultural exhibitions. Our terminal showcases Nigeria's rich heritage through rotating displays and live performances.

Use our real-time flight tracker on the homepage, download our mobile app for push notifications, or call our flight information hotline at +234 1 234 5679. We provide proactive updates for delays and gate changes.

We provide immediate notifications, rebooking assistance, meal vouchers for extended delays, and accommodation if needed. Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist with alternative arrangements and compensation claims.
