Cultural Intelligence

Understand Nigeria's rich cultural diversity and navigate social interactions with confidence and respect

Cultural Insights

1

Greetings & Respect

Nigerians value respect and courtesy. Use titles when addressing elders or professionals. Handshakes are common, but wait for women to extend their hand first.

2

Time & Punctuality

While "African time" is often referenced, business meetings increasingly expect punctuality. Arrive on time for formal appointments.

3

Communication Style

Nigerians appreciate directness balanced with diplomacy. Building relationships is crucial before conducting business.

4

Religious Considerations

Nigeria is religiously diverse. Be respectful of prayer times and religious holidays. Friday prayers and Sunday services are important.
